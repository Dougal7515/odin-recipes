Jiggs Dinner

Descripton

Traditional Newfoundland boiled dinner. All ingredients go in one pot and boiled for different durations. All finish cooking at the same time and served with gravy. Can be served with a second protein such as roast, turkey, chicken, etc.

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Turnip
  • Carrot
  • Cabbage
  • Salt Beef
  • Split Peas
  • Pickled Beets
  • Mustard pickles
  • Flour

Steps

  1. Soak split peas over night in pot of water.
  2. Boil pot of water, drain and put peas in pudding bag, secure bag and add to boiling water.
  3. Add salt beef and cook for two hours
  4. Add cabbage
  5. Add turnip and carrots
  6. Potatoes
  7. Scoop out vegetables
  8. Mash peas add butter salt and pepper
  9. Make gravey
  10. Serve
