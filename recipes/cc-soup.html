Cheddar Cheese Soup

Descripton

A delectable creamy soup with bacon, cheese, onion, celery and little else. This soup is a staple at Lecellier, a restaurant in the Canada Pavillion at Epcot, Disney World. It is best eaten not with a spoon but with pretzel breadsticks.

Ingredients

  • Extra Old White Cheddar Cheese
  • Medium Red Onion
  • Chicken Stock
  • Celery Ribs
  • Bacon
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Chives
  • Beer
  • Tabasco Sauce

Steps

  1. Fry bacon in pot. When browned add celery and onion.
  2. Add Flour stir constantly. Form a rue.
  3. Add Chicken Stock. Simmer stir.
  4. Once thickened add Milk
  5. Add Cheese stir until melted.
  6. Add salt and pper to taste.
  7. Add tabasco and beer.
  8. Use submersion blender to grind up any bits of veg or bacon remaining.
  9. Serve topped with crushed bacon and chopped chives.
  10. If available serve with pretzel bread sticks.
