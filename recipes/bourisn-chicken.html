Bourisn Chicken

Descripton

A creamy pasta sauce made with Boursin cheese and cubed chicken. Recipe will serve four people easily and take 30 mins to prep and cook.

Ingredients

  • Borisn Cheese
  • Penne Pasta
  • Whole Milk
  • Garlic
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Boneless Skinless Chickin Breast
  • Onion
  • Chive
  • Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

  1. Dice Onion Put Aside
  2. Cube Chicken
  3. Add oil to pan and heat. Once hot add oil and onion. Cook until onion is done.
  4. Add chicken to pan. Cook until done.
  5. Boil water turn pan on med-high
  6. Add Pasta cook for 10 mins
  7. Add milk to pan. very low simmer. Add borisn cheese. Stir constantly. Once melted add parm. Stir until smooth.
  8. Add pasta to sauce in pan. Add some pasta water. Stir until velevty and coating pasta.
  9. Serve and garnish with choped chives and shredded parm.
